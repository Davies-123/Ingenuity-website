About Ingenuity Tuition Center

Ingenuity Tuition Center was established to help learners achieve academic success through one-on-one tutoring and in-class sessions.

With our experienced tutors and teachers, we believe that every student has the potential to excel when provided with the right resources and guidance. At Ingenuity, we aim to produce students who excel academically and contribute to making Zambia a better place.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help students build strong academic foundations, improve confidence and academic expression, and perform extremely well in their final examinations. We believe in educating all for a better Zambia.

Our Teaching Approach

At Ingenuity, we make learning as accessible as possible. Students can learn from home through online sessions, and we also offer physical lessons at our site. Depending on their pace, students can choose one-on-one sessions or group learning with friends.
